About

I'm an Electrical Engineer based in the Philadelphia area with over 7 years of hands-on experience keeping complex electrical and mechanical systems running at peak performance. My background spans Amazon fulfillment centers, utility infrastructure, and GMP-regulated pharmaceutical environments.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering Technology from NJIT and carry certifications in OSHA 10 and NFPA 70E electrical safety. I specialize in troubleshooting 3-phase electrical systems, VFDs, motor controls, conveyor automation, and building infrastructure.

Outside of engineering, I'm deeply involved in live event and AV production — operating PTZ camera systems, video switchers, audio systems, and live streaming setups for church and venue environments. I bring the same technical precision to both worlds.
